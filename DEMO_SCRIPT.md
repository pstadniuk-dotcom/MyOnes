## 4. Health Profile (/dashboard/profile)

**Click Profile in the sidebar.**

> "The profile page has two tabs. The first is **personal info** — name, email, phone, address. The second is **Health** — this is where the magic starts."

**[SHOW]** Click the Health tab and scroll through the sections:

> "The health profile captures:
> - **Demographics** — age, sex, weight, height
> - **Vitals** — blood pressure, resting heart rate, sleep hours
> - **Lifestyle** — exercise frequency, stress level, smoking and alcohol status
> - **Medical History** — health conditions, medications, and allergies, all as tag inputs you can add and remove
>
> Everything you enter here feeds directly into the AI. The more complete your profile, the more personalized your formula."

## 5. Lab Reports (/dashboard/lab-reports)

**Navigate to Labs in the sidebar.**

> "Users can upload blood test PDFs directly. Our AI parses the document, extracts every biomarker, and generates a full analysis."

**[SHOW]** Point out the biomarker dashboard (if lab data exists):

> "After processing, you get:
> - An **overall health score** graded A through F with trend arrows
> - **Panel scores** — cardiovascular, metabolic, immune, thyroid, liver, kidney — each graded individually
> - **Focus areas** — the panels that need attention, with specific markers flagged and lifestyle recommendations
> - A **detailed biomarker table** — every marker with its value, reference range, trend over time, and status badge: Normal, High, Low, or Critical
>
> Click any marker to see its full history charted over time. The AI uses all of this when building your formula."
