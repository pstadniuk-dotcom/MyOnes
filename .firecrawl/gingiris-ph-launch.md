# Product Hunt Launch (PH Launch)

[Permalink: Product Hunt Launch (PH Launch)](https://github.com/Gingiris/gingiris-launch/blob/main/references/en/ph-launch.md#product-hunt-launch-ph-launch)

## Timing Considerations

[Permalink: Timing Considerations](https://github.com/Gingiris/gingiris-launch/blob/main/references/en/ph-launch.md#timing-considerations)

| Factor | Recommendation |
| :-- | :-- |
| Product Maturity | Ensure stability for traffic surge |
| Community Base | Have Discord/Telegram community ready |
| GitHub Stars | Recommend 500+ before PH launch |
| Competition | Avoid launching same day as well-known products |
| Day of Week | Tuesday-Thursday best; Monday competitive, weekend low traffic |

**Tool:** [PH Deck](https://www.phdeck.com/) — Check daily featured products

## Hunter Selection

[Permalink: Hunter Selection](https://github.com/Gingiris/gingiris-launch/blob/main/references/en/ph-launch.md#hunter-selection)

| Type | Pros | Cons |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| **Top Hunter** (e.g., Chris Messina) | High followers, strong endorsement | Expensive, busy schedule |
| **Mid-tier Hunter** | Good value, smooth communication | Less influence |
| **Local Hunter** | Low communication cost, affordable | Limited overseas influence |

### Hunter Collaboration Flow

[Permalink: Hunter Collaboration Flow](https://github.com/Gingiris/gingiris-launch/blob/main/references/en/ph-launch.md#hunter-collaboration-flow)

1. **Book** (L-6 weeks): Twitter DM or email
2. **Meeting** (L-4 weeks): 30-60 min video call, get feedback
3. **Draft Page** (L-2 weeks): Create draft, invite Hunter review
4. **Maker Profiles** (L-1 week): Ensure all team members have complete profiles
5. **Notify** (Day before): Click "Notify Followers"

## Upvote Strategy

[Permalink: Upvote Strategy](https://github.com/Gingiris/gingiris-launch/blob/main/references/en/ph-launch.md#upvote-strategy)

> ⚠️ **Important:** Product Hunt has strict rules against artificial vote manipulation. All upvote activity must come from genuine users who have tried or are interested in your product. Tactics that coordinate votes from users who have not used the product, or that use multiple accounts/IPs to simulate organic activity, violate PH's guidelines and can result in vote removal or account bans.

### Driving Genuine Upvotes

[Permalink: Driving Genuine Upvotes](https://github.com/Gingiris/gingiris-launch/blob/main/references/en/ph-launch.md#driving-genuine-upvotes)

The best way to rank highly on Product Hunt is to build a real community of users before launch day. Focus on:

| Channel | Best Practice |
| :-- | :-- |
| Existing community | Notify your Discord/Telegram/newsletter subscribers — they are your most genuine supporters |
| Social media | Share your PH page on Twitter/LinkedIn with an honest, compelling message |
| Personal network | Reach out personally to friends, colleagues, and advisors who have genuinely used the product |
| Email list | Send a launch-day email to your subscriber list with a direct link to your PH page |

### Contingency Plans

[Permalink: Contingency Plans](https://github.com/Gingiris/gingiris-launch/blob/main/references/en/ph-launch.md#contingency-plans)

| Situation | Response |
| :-- | :-- |
| Not Featured | Contact [support@producthunt.com](mailto:support@producthunt.com) |
| Pre-launched by others | Contact PH, provide ownership proof |
| Votes removed | Review your promotion strategy for any activity that may have appeared inauthentic; focus on genuine community engagement |
