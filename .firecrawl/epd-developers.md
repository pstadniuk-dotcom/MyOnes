## Additional Easy Pay Direct Platform Benefits

![](https://www.easypaydirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/integrations-small.png)

#### Hundreds Of Integrations In One

When you integrate with Easy Pay Direct’s gateway, you’re integrating with every major processing platform and payment provider in North America – and an ever expanding network outside. This means total flexibility with Merchant Accounts. Never be tied into one provider ever again.

[Learn More](https://www.easypaydirect.com/developers/hundreds-of-integrations-in-one/?)

#### Global Reach

Wherever you’re accepting payments, the experience should be simple and smooth – for you and your customer.

With Dynamic Currency Conversion, you can accept payments in any country and your customers can accept the payments in their local currency.

[Learn More](https://www.easypaydirect.com/merchant-accounts/high-risk-credit-card-processing/offshore/)

![](https://www.easypaydirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/APPROVED-CTA.webp)

#### Automated Approvals

Unlike PayPal or Stripe – We underwrite merchant accounts.  We do this so they don’t lose the ability to accept credit cards. Traditionally, that process slows things down… until now.We’ve put together a series of automated underwriting checks that will approve certain industry types in _real time._ A HUGE benefit when using Easy Pay Direct as a Payment Platform.

[Learn More](https://www.easypaydirect.com/automated-approvals/?)

#### Integrated Onboarding

Collect as few or as many fields as you’d like – and pass the data to our streamlined online application.  We’ll take it from there.

Easy for you.  Easy for Merchants.

[Learn More](https://www.easypaydirect.com/developers/integrated-onboarding/?)
