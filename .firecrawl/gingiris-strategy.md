## Four Pillars of Viral Launch

[Permalink: Four Pillars of Viral Launch](https://github.com/Gingiris/gingiris-launch/blob/main/references/en/strategy.md#four-pillars-of-viral-launch)

### Pillar 1: Product Power is the Foundation

[Permalink: Pillar 1: Product Power is the Foundation](https://github.com/Gingiris/gingiris-launch/blob/main/references/en/strategy.md#pillar-1-product-power-is-the-foundation)

Official materials should be restrained, focusing on product capabilities and benchmarks. Launch Video must be **"impressive enough" and "reproducible by users"**.

> **Case Study:** Manus's success came from product design that was perfect for screen recording — users could see the computer running with 50 windows open. This authenticity amplified the excitement.

### Pillar 2: Emotional Narrative Evolution

[Permalink: Pillar 2: Emotional Narrative Evolution](https://github.com/Gingiris/gingiris-launch/blob/main/references/en/strategy.md#pillar-2-emotional-narrative-evolution)

Successful launches achieve a leap from product narrative to emotional narrative:

- **Manus:** "First autonomous AI Agent" → **"Second Deepseek Moment"**
- **Devin:** "First AI software engineer" → **"The end of programmers"**

### Pillar 3: Influencer Flywheel Effect

[Permalink: Pillar 3: Influencer Flywheel Effect](https://github.com/Gingiris/gingiris-launch/blob/main/references/en/strategy.md#pillar-3-influencer-flywheel-effect)

Cold-start always depends on entrepreneurs, executives, tech experts, and investors with high-quality "real followers". They are the **"true ignitors of cold-start traffic"**:

1. Not mega-influencers, can express genuine emotions
2. High-quality audience, "one is worth ten"
3. Natural endorsement from their identity

### Pillar 4: Platform Relay Race

[Permalink: Pillar 4: Platform Relay Race](https://github.com/Gingiris/gingiris-launch/blob/main/references/en/strategy.md#pillar-4-platform-relay-race)

| Phase | Timing | Main Platforms | User State |
| :-- | :-- | :-- | :-- |
| **Topic Creation** | Day 0-3 | X, WeChat Moments | FOMO, "something big is happening" |
| **Content Sedimentation** | Day 3-10 | YouTube, Substack | Active search, converted by deep content |
| **Long-tail Traffic** | Day 10+ | Reddit, HN | Deep users, sustained traffic |
