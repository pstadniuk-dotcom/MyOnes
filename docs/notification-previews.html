SMS

Daily Pill Reminders

Capsules are split evenly across active meal reminders. Example: 9 capsules ÷ 3 meals = 3 per meal. Sent at the user's configured breakfast, lunch, and dinner times in their timezone.

Trigger: smsReminderScheduler.ts → sendReminderSms() (cron every minute)

Requires: sms_accountability consent + dailyRemindersEnabled
8:00 AM ONES 📶 🔋
ONES
+1 (855) 389-0981
💊 Breakfast time! Take 3 capsules with your meal.
8:00 AM · User's breakfast time
💊 Lunch time! Take 3 capsules with your meal.
12:00 PM · User's lunch time
💊 Dinner time! Take 3 capsules with your meal.
6:00 PM · User's dinner time
How splitting works:
Total capsules from formula's targetCapsules ÷ active meal slots.

Examples:
• 9 caps, 3 meals → 3 per meal
• 6 caps, 3 meals → 2 per meal
• 12 caps, 2 meals → 6 per meal

Timing: Follows user's reminderBreakfast, reminderLunch, reminderDinner settings in their timezone. Empty time = no reminder for that meal.
